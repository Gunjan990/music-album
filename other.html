Making Memories

KARAN AUJLA

He is an Indian singer, rapper, and songwriter in the Punjabi music industry. He has released over fifty-two singles as a lead artist and thirty-four as a lyricist. He has also featured on fifty-four songs as a guest artist. Eleven of his singles as a lead artist have been featured on the global YouTube music chart, while nineteen have been featured on the UK Asian music chart by the OCC.

On Making Memories, Punjabi folk vocal melodies and desi lyrics blend seamlessly with a lush, expansive pop sound rooted in contemporary soul, R&B, and hip-hop.
